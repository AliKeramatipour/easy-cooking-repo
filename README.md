# 🍽️ Easy Cooking Repo

A collection of simple, quick, and delicious recipes designed for busy people who want to eat well without spending hours in the kitchen.

## What's Inside

Recipes that are:
- ⏱️ **Quick** — Most dishes ready in 30 minutes or less
- 🙌 **Minimal effort** — Straightforward methods, no unnecessary steps
- 💷 **Budget-friendly** — Affordable ingredients
- ✨ **Actually tasty** — No compromises on flavor

## How to Use

Browse the recipes and pick one that sounds good. Each recipe includes ingredients, method, serving suggestions, and cost estimates.

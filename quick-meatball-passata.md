# Quick Meatball Passata

**Time:** 30 min | **Effort:** 5 min hands-on | **Serves:** 1

## Ingredients

- Raw meatballs (4-6)
- 1 jar passata
- Handful mozzarella (grated or torn)
- Salt, pepper, olive oil

## Method

1. Dump meatballs into an oven-proof dish.
2. Pour passata over them. Add 2 tbsp water.
3. Bake at 190 °C for 25 min.
4. Top with mozzarella, bake 5 more min until melted.

## Serve with

- Crusty bread (dunk in sauce)
- Microwave rice pouch
- Polenta (best option — sauce soaks in beautifully)

~£2–2.50 per serving
